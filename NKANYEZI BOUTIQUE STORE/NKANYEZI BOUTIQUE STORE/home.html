About Ubungoma

Ubungoma is a traditional African spiritual practice, mainly among the Zulu, Xhosa, and other Nguni-speaking people in South Africa. It involves divination, healing, and communicating with ancestors. A person who practices ubungoma is called an isangoma.

Role of an Isangoma

  • Healing: Isangomas heal physical, emotional, and spiritual illnesses.
  • Divination: They help clients understand the cause of problems by reading bones, shells, or other objects.
  • Guidance: They advise on love, career, business, family, and protection from negative forces.
  • Spiritual Connection: Isangomas act as intermediaries between the living and ancestral spirits (amadlozi/abaphansi).
